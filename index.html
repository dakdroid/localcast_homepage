Supported devices

Chromecast, Nexus Player, Smart TVs, Sonos, Xbox and a lot more DLNA/Upnp devices...

LoaclCast supports thousands of devices. If your device isn't supported yet, let me know!

UI

The beautiful material user interface let's you find your media in a second.

Touch the cast button to connect to your device, then choose what to cast. That easy!

Sources

LocalCast has a built in file browser and a library view. You can even browse your NAS, Google Drive or Google Plus.

If you want to watch a web stream, just share the link with LocalCast or choose LocalCast to open the link.
